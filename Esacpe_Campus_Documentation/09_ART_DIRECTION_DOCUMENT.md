# ART PHILOSOPHY

Escape Campus bukan horror tentang monster.

Escape Campus adalah horror tentang:

- Penyesalan
- Tekanan akademik
- Ketakutan gagal
- Kehilangan arah hidup

Visual harus membuat pemain merasa:

"Tempat ini dulu normal."

"Tapi sekarang ada sesuatu yang salah."

# SHADOW RULES

Avoid complete darkness.

Player must always see enough to move.

---

Horror should come from:

What player sees.

Not what player cannot see.

# HORROR DISTORTION DESIGN

Distortion should feel:

Possible.

Then impossible.

---

Example:

Normal classroom.

---

One chair moved.

---

Three chairs moved.

---

All chairs facing player.

---

This progression creates tension.
