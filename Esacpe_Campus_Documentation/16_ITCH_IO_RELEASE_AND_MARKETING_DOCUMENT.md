# ITCH.IO STORE PAGE
########################################################

Store Name

Escape Campus

---

Short Description

A psychological horror game about a student trapped inside a campus that refuses to let anyone graduate.

---

Long Description

Escape Campus is a first-person psychological horror experience set inside an abandoned university at night.

You arrive to finish your final thesis revision before tomorrow's defense.

What should have been a quick visit becomes a nightmare when the campus enters a reality-bending academic loop.

Investigate forgotten records.

Solve increasingly disturbing puzzles.

Uncover the truth behind a student who vanished before graduation.

And escape before the campus claims you too.
